BUG SIMULATOR

A vugg-simulator sibling. The cavity is now a niche at insect scale — a rotting log, a pitcher plant, a vertebrate carcass. Sessile organisms (mosses, fungi, lichens, algae) nucleate on the substrate. Motile bug agents colonize, hunt, and breed on top.

See proposals/PROPOSAL-BUG-SIMULATOR.md for the design brief and reading order.

Status: v1 scaffold — build pipeline + empty engine. No species, no scenarios, no renderer yet. The next session adds the first scenario (Białowieża beech log, year 5) and its four-species saproxylic core.
